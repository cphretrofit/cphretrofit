A Brief History of CPH Retrofit

CPH Retrofit was established to respond to the growing need for high-quality, practical retrofit solutions that improve building performance while remaining commercially realistic. From the outset, the company has focused on delivering whole-house and fabric-first retrofit approaches that align with evolving standards, carbon-reduction targets, and the real-world constraints of existing buildings.

Working across residential and mixed-use projects, CPH Retrofit has developed a reputation for combining technical understanding with clear project delivery, supporting clients through assessment, design coordination, and on-site implementation. The company's approach is rooted in collaboration, quality assurance, and long-term building performance rather than short-term fixes.

1.2 What Does a PAS 2030/2035 Retrofit Business Do?

‚ñº

The core services of the business are Retrofit Assessment, Coordination and Design. Our primary client base are social housing providers and councils throughout England.

A PAS 2030/2035 retrofit services business plans, designs, manages, and delivers energy-efficiency improvements to existing homes, in a way that is safe, compliant, and genuinely effective.

It's not just about fitting insulation or heat pumps. It's about making homes warmer, cheaper to run, and lower-carbon without causing damage.

Our Process

  • Complete extensive pre-installation surveys (Retrofit Assessments) tailored to each home's needs
  • Prioritise resident comfort and highest standards of quality
  • Maintain transparent communication ‚Äì residents kept fully informed at every stage
  • Install measures (air source heat pumps, solar PV, loft insulation, ventilation systems) according to manufacturer guidance and industry best practice
  • Minimize disruption and prevent unintended consequences
  • Rigorous quality checks and ongoing resident feedback
